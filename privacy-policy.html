Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Muslim Ai ("we", "us", "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application on Android and iOS. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location

  • We request access to your device's location (GPS) to calculate accurate prayer times and determine Qibla direction.
  • Your location is used only on your device to fetch daily prayer timings from a public Islamic data provider.
  • We do not store, sell, or share your GPS coordinates with any third party.

1.2 Notification Preferences

  • If you allow notifications, the app schedules local reminders for prayer times and daily du'as directly on your device.
  • No notification content or schedule is sent to our servers.

1.3 Optional Account (Email)

  • The app offers an optional sign-in to sync bookmarks and tasbih counts across devices.
  • If you sign up, we store only your email address and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in readable form.

1.4 Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID) and approximate location for ad personalization. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time from your device's settings.
See Google's AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

2. Third-Party Services

  • Aladhan API — fetches prayer times from your approximate coordinates.
  • Al-Quran Cloud API — serves the Qur'an text & translations.
  • Google AdMob — ad delivery.

3. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections for all network requests. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt). We follow reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security over the internet.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is rated for ages 3+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Your Rights

  • Disable location access at any time in your device settings — prayer times will fall back to a default city.
  • Disable notifications at any time from the app Settings screen or device settings.
  • Request account deletion by emailing us (see contact below).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

7. Contact
